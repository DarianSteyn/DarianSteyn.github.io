Icebreak

Application Design

Year 2016

SUMMARY

Icebreak was a messenger application concept which I created and designed. The user is able to create a messaging profile for themself on their device. They can then detect the nearest users of Icebreak, which they can send a chat request to if they were interested in that users profile. The receiving user will then either approve or deny the request. The concept was based on the idea of helping people break the ice with other users that are in viewing range.
